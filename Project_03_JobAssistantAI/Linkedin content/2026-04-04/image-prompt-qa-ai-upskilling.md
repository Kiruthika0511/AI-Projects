# Universal Image Prompt — "The QA Engineer Who Learns AI Will Outlast the One Who Doesn't"
**Theme:** Career growth through AI upskilling.
**Banner Text:** GROW WITH AI. GROW YOUR CAREER.

## UNIVERSAL IMAGE PROMPT (Copy for DALL·E / Midjourney / Firefly)

Warm cartoon illustration with thick black outlines and chibi proportions. The main character is a South Asian female QA engineer with shoulder-length black hair and an optimistic, confident expression. She is seated at a professional desk that is visually split into two halves. 

The left side represents the "Past": muted tones, stacks of old manual test scripts, and an analog clock. The right side represents the "Future": vibrant golden tones, glowing AI neural network lines, and a rising line graph indicating career growth. 

**Background:** A warm golden-yellow gradient with subtle, glowing circuit board and tech patterns. 
**Banner:** A bold, semi-transparent text banner at the top reads: "GROW WITH AI. GROW YOUR CAREER." 
**Details:** The character wears a red graphic t-shirt and colorful woven bracelets.

### CROP RATIO SPECIFICATIONS:
- **16:9 (YouTube/Medium):** Full desk view showing the contrast between the manual scripts on the left and the AI nodes on the right.
- **1:1 (LinkedIn/Instagram):** Centered on the character and the rising growth graph, keeping the banner clearly visible.
- **4:5 (Portrait Social):** Tight focus on the character's face and the "Right Side" (AI growth elements), highlighting the text banner.
