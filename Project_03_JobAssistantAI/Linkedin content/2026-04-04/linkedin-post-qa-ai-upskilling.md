QA engineers with AI skills earn 28% more than those without.

Job postings requiring AI knowledge in QA tripled between 2022 and 2024.

Same role. Same title. Completely different outcome.

The data is not predicting the future — it is describing the present. LinkedIn's 2024 Jobs on the Rise report placed AI-adjacent SDET roles among the fastest growing in tech. Engineers who list LLM testing or AI agent pipelines on their profiles sit materially above the salary baseline.

Most QA engineers are not resisting AI. They are just waiting for their company to mandate it. That mandate will come too late.

The question is whether you move on your terms or theirs.
